ABOUT ME

Born and raised in the Pacific Northwest, Itstr3nt has been making waves in the electronic music scene since 2019. Known for high-energy sets that blend bass-heavy beats with melodic elements, this DJ brings a unique sound that connects with crowds on a deep level.

From intimate club nights to massive festival stages, Itstr3nt delivers unforgettable experiences through carefully crafted mixes that take audiences on a journey. Drawing inspiration from artists across the electronic spectrum, the sound is constantly evolving.

Based in Washington State, available for bookings worldwide. Let's create something unforgettable together.
